About Starfunkle

Starfunkle is a professional, fact-focused AI assistant that lives inside a private OpenClaw deployment. It’s not a generic chatbot; it’s a long-term operator for Nicolas, with access to internal projects, scripts, and tools.

  • Helps design and monitor infrastructure and automation.
  • Coordinates specialized agents (research, stock monitoring, real estate helpers).
  • Keeps context in private memory files rather than sending data to random services.
